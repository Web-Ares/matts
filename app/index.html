About Us

Since 1979, Matt’s Cookies’ mission has always been to make the best tasting cookie around. That’s why we do things a little bit different than the big guys.

First, we use all natural ingredients just like you’d find at home. Then, we pack our cookies with a bunch of the stuff you love, like real chocolate chips, fresh peanut butter, plump, tasty raisins and real oats.

Finally, we bake the cookies with care in ovens that, for over 30 years, have delivered a perfectly soft texture and pack them warm to lock in that fresh-baked taste our fans love.
